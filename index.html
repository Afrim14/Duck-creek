Our Story

Family Owned Since 1985

Duck Creek Pancake House has been serving the Quad Cities area with delicious homemade breakfast and lunch for over 35 years. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into one of the area's most beloved dining establishments.

We take pride in using only the freshest ingredients and preparing each dish with care. Our pancakes are made from scratch daily, and our recipes have been passed down through generations.

Whether you're joining us for a hearty breakfast, a business lunch, or a weekend family meal, our friendly staff will make you feel right at home.
